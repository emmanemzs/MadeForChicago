WHAT IS THIS
GUIDE?

This guide is your go-to resource for a perfect day in Chicago, featuring my personal favorite food spots, a cozy cafe, charming boutiques, and a must-visit at the Art Institute of Chicago. Whether you're planning a day trip or looking to rediscover the city, these recommendations showcase the best of what Chicago has to offer.

COFFEE

SOLOWAY COFFEE

224 N Michigan Ave

Soloway Coffee is a café in Lincoln Park that serves coffee, pastries, and breakfast all day. It’s a good place to stop for a quick coffee, a snack, or a meal. The café has a relaxed atmosphere and is great for casual visits.

Chicago
ARTS AND
CULTURE

The Chicago Art Institute

The Art Institute of Chicago is a great place to visit if you enjoy art or just want a quiet, interesting way to spend some time. The museum has a mix of famous pieces and lesser-known works, so there’s something for everyone. You can easily spend hours wandering through its galleries, discovering new favorites and learning about different styles and histories of art.
